Our Mission

Connecting Uganda's Construction Industry

Uganda's construction industry is booming — new roads, new estates, new buildings everywhere. But finding the right truck driver, excavator operator, hardware supplier or professional still happens through phone calls and word of mouth.

Bonki SiteLink was built to change that. We created a simple, affordable digital platform where any provider — from a single truck owner in Villages to a hardware shop in Kampala — can list their services and get found by clients across the whole country.

Our mission is to digitize Uganda's construction supply chain, create income opportunities for thousands of providers and make building in Uganda faster, easier and more reliable for everyone.
